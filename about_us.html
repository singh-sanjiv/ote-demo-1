ABOUT US

World-class materials and machinery

DEMO-OTE-WEBSITE Inc. is a leading trading company specializing in supplying the ceramic industry with a comprehensive range of equipment and materials. We offer a complete solution, from raw materials to finished product, catering to the diverse needs of our clients.

Established in 2014, DEMO-OTE-WEBSITE International Pvt. Ltd. embarked on a mission to elevate the Indian ceramic industry to global standards. We recognized the immense potential within Indian tile manufacturers, but also identified a gap in the quality of raw materials and machinery hindering their competitive edge.

Sourcing the Finest Raw Materials

Uplifting Quality Standards and Production

Driven by this vision, we embarked on a relentless pursuit of sourcing the finest raw materials and machinery from across the globe. We established strategic partnerships with leading manufacturers in Italy, Spain, and China, bringing cutting-edge inks, additives, chemicals, and polishing lines to the Indian market.

As a result, DEMO-OTE-WEBSITE International has become a recognized leader in introducing the best technology and materials available globally to the Indian ceramic industry. We are proud to play a pivotal role in uplifting the quality standards and production output of our clients, empowering them to compete effectively on the international stage.

Our Mission

To empower ceramic businesses by providing them with the highest quality of Inks, additives, chemicals and machinery, along with exceptional customer service. We strive to be a trusted partner in their success, helping them achieve optimal efficiency, productivity, and innovation.
